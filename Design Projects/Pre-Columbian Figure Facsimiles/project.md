### Overview
For this project, I collaborated with artists, Nasher museum staff, Duke faculty, and technologists to test workflows for digitizing fragile Pre-Columbian figures. I helped with scanning setup and capture, evaluated tradeoffs between different scanning approaches, and supported model cleanup so the geometry could be prepared for fabrication.

From those scans, I helped produce test prints to evaluate how well the forms translated into physical facsimiles. The work focused on making artifacts more accessible for teaching and artistic use while respecting the integrity of the original objects.

From these scans we have constructed facsimiles of several precolombian works that are very difficult to distinguish from the originals.
