### Overview
For this project, I supported the 3D printing of heart models used in pediatric cardiology surgeries. I helped prepare and produce prints that gave clinical teams clearer physical references for case planning.

The work focused on reliable fabrication workflows and print quality so the models could be delivered on time and used effectively in surgical preparation.
