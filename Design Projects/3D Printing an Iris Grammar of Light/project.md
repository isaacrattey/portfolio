### Overview
This project produced a 3D printed iris flower for an art installation. I guided the student in using **Blender** for modifying the 3D model of an iris. I consulted with her to advise her about the best 3D printer to use for production, and the steps for postprocessing to achieve the desired appearance. The outcome was a completed first-pass artifact commissioned by Duke's 2023 MFA and EDA cohort, providing a foundation for continued exploration of light, color, and form.
