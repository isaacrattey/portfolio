### Overview
When we had the opportunity to improve our workflow, we spent time designing tools to organize our Bluesmith space. This helped us speed up turnaround time and increase reliability of our services. Here were some projects that had an impact in our lab:

- **IPA Holders**: Shelf mounted IPA holders
- **F370 Tray Rack**: 3D printed fixture for hanging build trays for the F370 printer
- **J750 Tool Organizer**: Organizer for IPA and water bottles and screwdrivers for J750 maintenance
- **Form 3/4 Cartridge Holders**: Cartridge holders for the Form 3 and Form 4 printers
- **Whiteboard Holder**: Wall mounted holder for markers and whiteboard

I mentored my student employees to work on these projects.
