### Overview
For this project, I supported an artist through fabrication and iteration of his artwork during his residency at Duke. His residency allowed him to reenvision his work to take advantage of the equipment offered at the Duke Co-Lab.

I worked closely with him to train him to use the equipment, and help him build his artwork with a short timeframe. The collaboration culminated in an exhibition in the Nasher art museum that was entirely made in the Co-Lab's makerspaces.
