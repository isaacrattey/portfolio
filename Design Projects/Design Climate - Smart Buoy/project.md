### Overview
We were asked to help build a solar-powered buoy that could collect ocean data to be relayed to an app. The buoy housed a Raspberry Pi 5 with remote connectivity and integrated a temperature sensor, camera, and accelerometer. The design used a two-part waterproof shell with a custom gasket, acrylic electronics plates, and external slots for solar panels.

I mentored my student employees to work on this project.
