### Overview
For this project, commissioned by artists at Duke, we were asked to design an easy-to-assemble box and source projection material. The box needed to be large enough for a performer to dance inside the box while graphics were projected on the outside of the box.

We designed a box using quick-frame material so that it could be disassembled with minimal required equipment and shipped overseas for performances across the world.

I mentored my student employees to work on this project.
