### Overview
For a Parkinson's Researcher, we designed a 3D printed holder to connect two "button boxes" for a CT scan study. These holders needed to be completely made of plastic so that they would not interfere with the CT scanner. This required us to be creative about how to connect the two halves without using any metal fasteners such as screws. We used clever 3D printed snap joints to attach the two halves.

I mentored my student employees to work on this project.
