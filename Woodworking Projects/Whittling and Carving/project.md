### Overview
This project highlights my whittling and carving work using hand tools. I practiced precision cuts, texture development, and finishing techniques while building comfort with fine detail and material control.

I enjoy carving because it gives me a greater understanding of grain structure, and the intricacies of working with wood, which I can apply when working on larger projects.
